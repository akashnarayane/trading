*Resistence: Highest high Highest close
*Suppore : Lowest low lowest close
*Area of basing : 
*RSI > 70 - Over bought (Expensive ) then Short sell
*RSi < 30 - Over Sold (Cheap) then buy
*For indian market 60 - Over bought and 40 - Over sold   is used.
*Divergences :
	Example of classic divergence.
	Prices : Lower low,   RSI : Higher low -> 	Bullish.    *(Stock should be over sold i.e, 	<40) .

	Prices : higher high, RSI : Lower high -> Bearish 
 *(Stock should be over sold i.e, >60) .
